Infant Care

Gentle, attentive care for your littlest ones in a warm, safe, and loving home environment.

About Our Infant Program

We understand how precious and fragile infants are. Our infant care program is built around trust, routine, and one-on-one attention. Your baby will be held, nurtured, and cared for as if they were our own. We follow safe sleep practices at all times and keep parents informed throughout the day.

What's Included

  • Individual attention and responsive caregiving
  • Tummy time and sensory development activities
  • Safe sleep practices (SIDS guidelines followed)
  • Daily updates for parents
  • Feeding support (breast milk, formula, or solid introduction)
  • Warm, home-like environment
  • Bilingual caregiving (English & Spanish)

Schedule & Availability

We offer flexible full-day and part-time schedules for infants. Spots are limited — contact us to check current availability and discuss a schedule that works for your family.
