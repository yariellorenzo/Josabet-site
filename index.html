Infant Care

We provide a warm, safe, and loving environment for infants from 6 weeks to 12 months. Our infant care program focuses on building trust, establishing routines, and encouraging early development through sensory play, tummy time, and one-on-one attention.

  • Ages: 6 weeks – 12 months
  • Small group size for individual attention
  • Daily activity reports for parents
  • Safe sleep practices followed at all times

Toddler Programs

Our toddler program is designed for children ages 1–3. We focus on building independence, language development, and social skills through structured play, arts and crafts, music, and outdoor activities.

  • Ages: 1 – 3 years
  • Language and motor skill development
  • Arts, music, and outdoor play
  • Potty training support

Preschool

Our preschool program prepares children ages 3–5 for kindergarten. We teach letters, numbers, shapes, colors, and social skills in a fun, engaging way that makes learning feel like play.

  • Ages: 3 – 5 years
  • Kindergarten readiness curriculum
  • Letters, numbers, shapes, and colors
  • Social and emotional learning
